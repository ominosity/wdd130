Oregon, USA

Oregon is located on the West Coast of the United States of America, sandwiched between California and Washington. It is a state of environmental contrasts and diversity. It boasts the contiguous United States only year-round ski resort (weather permitting) on a volcano (Mount Hood), but also contains endless miles of desert and an impressive Pacific Coast.
