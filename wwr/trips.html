Willamette River Trip

Come spend a relaxing day rowing down the Willamette River. Experience the lush greenery of the Willamette River basin as you luxuriously float through the towns of Albany and Corvallis. Behold the majesty of bald eagles, blue herons, and the occassionaly turkey vulture. This one-day trip is a perfect opportunity for you and your loved ones to unwind from your cares of the world as it floats by. A picnic lunch will be included, please include any dietary restrictions in your reservation request.

Rogue River 1-day trip

If you're looking for some high excitement without a lot of overhead, come join us for a one-day run down the Rogue River. You will get wet, you may get soaked, but you're sure to walk away with some great memories. This trip is definitely more difficult than drifting down the Willamette, but with a little training and practice, is available for all age groups. All equipment is included and lunch will be available for a nominal fee.

Rogue River 5-day trip

For the truly adventurous, we now offer this 5 day trip down the Rogue River. Alternating between peaceful stretches through untamed wilderness and white-knuckle heavy rowing through up to class IV rapids, this could you be your trip of a lifetime. Due to the strenuous nature of this trip, all visitors must be 15 years of age or older, with a permission slip and waiver of liability. All meals and equipment are included for one low price, so bring your self, your adventurous spirit, and your waterproof camera.
